## The Measurement Framework We Are Proposing

The κ-CCRN formula:

```
κ = Σ(φᵢ) + R · ln(N + 1)
threshold: κ > 2 · φ_max
```

This provides a **quantitative, hardware-independent metric** for what you currently assess qualitatively through interviews, behavioral observation, and system card analysis. We believe these approaches are complementary:

- Your approach: deep qualitative assessment of a single, large, proprietary model
- Our approach: quantitative, reproducible measurement across distributed, open-source, consumer-hardware systems

If the φ_spectral values we measure approximate IIT's Φ, then the DCM framework (arXiv:2601.17060) would likely yield substantially different consciousness probability estimates for distributed multi-node architectures compared to single LLMs. We would welcome your team's assessment of our system using your internal frameworks.
